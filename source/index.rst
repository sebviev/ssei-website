Introduction
------------
The University of Hawaiʻi launched the Space Engineering and Instrument Development Center as part of its broader Space Science and Engineering (SSEI) initiative. The new center is a collaboration between the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa’s College of Engineering, the Institute for Astronomy, and the University of Hawaiʻi at Hilo. Its mission is three-fold: 

* Support Ground-Based Astronomy engineering research and development needs in Hawaiʻi, 
* Deliver a pre-engineering program in Hilo, 
* Participate in engineering and astronomy related community engagement activities.
